Purple sweet potato

Homepage

Description

There are many varieties of purple tubers, but this is specifically the purple japanese sweet potato. The vibrant purple color indicates the antioxidant content, and is very healthy for you.

Ingredients

  • Purple sweet potato
  1. Wrap the sweet potato in a baking sheet. Then wrap in aluminum foil.
  2. Place in oven at 180 degrees celsius. for 90mins.
  3. Remove from oven and let cool.
